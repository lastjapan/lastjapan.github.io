Jam Baxter 'Touching Scenes' Album

I produced 'Vulture' (track 12) on Jam Baxter's 'Touching Scenes' album released November 2018 on High Focus Records. The track was also released on a limited edition 7" vinyl, hand numbered and signed by Jam Baxter together with the track Saliva. Jam wanted to explore something that was outside the norm of his current work. My aim was to keep to my roots in grime but also include elements Jam Baxter fans could relate with his past work.


COLD ASCENT

A three track body of work created in collaboration with Finnish producer Inner. As the first release on the label ASCEND we came together to fuse influences from our own tastes in Drill, Grime, and Jungle. Artwork by Dylan Tushar.


LUNA

Released in March 2018, LUNA featured eight tracks. The 12" vinyl version of LUNA featured a 12x24" pull out black and white poster. Drawing inspiration from astronomy, LUNA was an alternative take on soundtrack to movies such as Interstellar and 2001: A Space Odyssey. The record featured guest vocals from artist Prynce Mini. All art direction and design was by Jase Coop.


ASICS 'What The Gel'

In the first ever global brand campaign for ASICSTIGER, I was asked to appear alongside up and coming French-Algerian hip hop artist Ta Ra and Ildar Iksanov, the Moscow based stylist in the ‘What The Gel’ campaign. Directed by photographer Robert Wunsch.


EXHALE / TEFLON

Exhale and Teflon released as a 7" vinyl run on Circadian Rhythms which came with a hand screen printed posted by William Green. Exhale featured London artist and MC Killa P with his signature yardie flow whilst on the b side Teflon was a personal experiment to create a piece of music that invoked the feeling of tension and anticipation.


Evian x KENZO 'Naturally Playful'

Nokia 'Lumia 820'

I was approached to be a part of this global campaign for Nokia by London based company DesignStudio. Together with Luke Oldham I created the music for and also starred in three seperate adverts that showcased various features of the Nokia Lumia 820.
